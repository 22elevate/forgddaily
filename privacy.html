Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how 22 Elevate ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Forgd Daily mobile application ("App"). By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide: Forgd Daily does not require you to create an account. The App operates locally on your device. Your saved favorites and preferences are stored only on your device using local storage and are never transmitted to our servers.

Subscription information: When you subscribe to Forgd Daily Premium, payment is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not store or have access to your payment details. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat may collect a pseudonymous user identifier and subscription status to verify your access.

Notification preferences: If you enable daily reminders, your chosen notification time is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to any server.

Usage data: We may receive anonymized, aggregated usage analytics from Apple and Google (such as crash reports and install counts). This data does not identify you personally.

2. How We Use Information

  • To deliver and improve the App experience
  • To verify your premium subscription status via RevenueCat
  • To send local push notifications if you have enabled them
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues via crash logs

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We work with the following service providers:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management. View their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Apple / Google — app distribution, payment processing, and optional analytics. See Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies for details.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your favorites, settings, and notification preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal content. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data transmitted to our service providers.

5. Children's Privacy

Forgd Daily is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete your data: You can delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Delete All My Data inside the App.
  • Notifications: You can disable or change your notification time at any time in the App or in your device's Settings.
  • Subscription: Manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID or Google Play account settings.

7. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
